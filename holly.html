Welcome to my webpage!

My webpage is all about the seabin, a floating rubbish bin that is located in the water at marinas, docks, yacht clubs and commercial ports.

The seabin moves up and down with the range of tide collecting all floating rubbish. Water is sucked from the surfaceand passes through a catch bag inside the seabin. With a submersible water pumpcapable of displacing 25,000 litres per hour plugged
